This application is to help any dungeon master ot make their campaigns even more immersive. Easily programable buttons and option to export all settings and audio files, gives flexability to use it on any device supporting modern browsers (sorry IE).

This application does not collect any data. Everything you do on it is actioned on your device (using browser cache). If you configure too many buttons, your device might not be able to store them in memory.
